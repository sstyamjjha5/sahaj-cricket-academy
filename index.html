FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Everything you need to know about joining SCCA.

What age groups do you coach at the academy?

We cater to all age groups. Our Development program starts at U-12 (Under 12 years), while our Competitive and High-Performance programs are designed for active league players of all ages up to senior levels.

Where is Sahaj Chadha Cricket Academy located?

We are primarily located in Noida, U.P., India. For exact ground locations and net practice venues, please reach out via WhatsApp or phone to book your initial trial.

Do you offer personal 1-on-1 coaching?

Yes. Our 'Personal Training' and 'High Performance' tiers are exclusively dedicated to one-on-one sessions. This includes video analysis, personalized diet plans, and advanced biomechanics correction directly with Coach Sahaj.
